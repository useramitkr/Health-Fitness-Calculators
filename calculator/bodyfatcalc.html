What is Body Fat Percentage?

Body fat percentage is the proportion of fat to total body weight. It is an important indicator of health and fitness.

Healthy Body Fat Ranges:

  • Essential Fat: 10-13% (women), 2-5% (men)
  • Athletes: 14-20% (women), 6-13% (men)
  • Fitness: 21-24% (women), 14-17% (men)
  • Average: 25-31% (women), 18-24% (men)
  • Obese: 32%+ (women), 25%+ (men)

Maintaining a healthy body fat percentage can help reduce the risk of health issues such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.
